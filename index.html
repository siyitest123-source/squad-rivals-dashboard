Methodology. Revenue is computed bottom-up from WAU, segment conversion rates, and CHZ-denominated fees converted at the configured CHZ price. Activation revenue assumes activating users pay for the configured number of slots each week. Flexibility revenue assumes ~3 purchases/week per buying user at the configured ticket price (a blended ticket/scout average). Assistant Manager assumes weekly subscription pricing. The 40/25/15/10/10 allocation splits gross revenue into Reward Pool / Platform / Buyback & Burn / Treasury Reserve / Acquisition.
