Methodology. Every revenue stream follows the same formula: weekly revenue = WAU × adoption rate × txns/week × unit price × platform take % × CHZ price. Streams are fully editable — rename them, adjust their levers, delete them, or add new ones. For Wager Commission, head-to-head challenge mechanics fold into the template: txns/week = wagers initiated × accept rate (matched within the time window), unit price = total matched pot (2× stake), and platform take = the commission cut. Scenario buttons (Bear/Base/Bull) set market conditions (WAU, CHZ price) while preserving your stream setup. The 40/25/15/10/10 allocation splits gross revenue into Reward Pool / Platform / Buyback & Burn / Treasury Reserve / Acquisition.
